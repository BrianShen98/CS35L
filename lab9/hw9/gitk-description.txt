1.upper parts
The upper window visualizes the whole history of commits, their relation 
the author of the commits, and the date of the commit. Also, it displays the
SHA1 ID of each commit.

2.lower parts
The lower left window shows the ChangeLog and actual content of the specific commit. 
We can choose to view the content in "Diff" "Old version" "New Version" modes.

The lowerright window shows the hierarchy of the commit. If in "Patch" mode,
it will show the hierarchy of the content in lower right window. If in "Tree"
mode, it will show the hierarchy of the tree of the commit.
